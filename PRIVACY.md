# Privacy summary — COC OMR

**App:** COC OMR (`edu.coc.omr`)  
**For:** Cagayan de Oro College teachers  

## What we store

| Data | On your phone | In the cloud (Supabase) |
|------|---------------|-------------------------|
| Teacher name, school, email | Yes | Yes (profile) |
| Offline PIN | Hashed on phone only | No |
| Class roster, student names | Yes | Yes (when you sync) |
| Exam scores and answers | Yes | Yes (when you sync) |
| Scan photos | Yes, on device only | **No** |

## Who can see it

- **You** — on your phone after PIN unlock
- **You** — in the cloud after sign-in (your account only)
- **Developers** — Supabase dashboard for support (not daily use)
- **Other teachers** — cannot see your data

## Your choices

- Use **offline PIN** after signing in (daily unlock without Wi‑Fi)
- **Sign out** clears local session; cloud data remains until you delete the account
- **Export JSON Backup** in Settings for your own copy

## Security

- Use a phone PIN and do not share your offline unlock PIN
- Sync on trusted school Wi‑Fi

## Contact

School IT or the app developer for data deletion requests.
