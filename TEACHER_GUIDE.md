# COC OMR — Teacher Quick Start

## First time (needs internet — required)

You **must** sign in or register once so your data is tied to your account and backed up when you sync.

1. Open **COC OMR** on your phone.
2. Tap **Register** (or **Sign in** if you already have an account).
3. Enter your email, password, name, and school.
4. Create a **4–6 digit PIN** — you will use this every exam day without Wi‑Fi.
5. You land on the **Dashboard**.

## Every exam day (no internet)

1. Open the app → enter your **PIN** on the unlock screen.
2. Go to **Classes** → pick a section → **Scan**.
3. Hold the sheet flat, good light, full page in frame.
4. If the app says **needs review**, tap **Review** and fix answers — do not guess.
5. Scores save on the phone automatically.

## After the exam (when Wi‑Fi is back)

1. Open **Settings** (bottom right).
2. Tap **Sync Now** so roster and scores upload to the cloud.
3. Optional: **Export JSON Backup** before you leave school.

## New phone

1. Sign in online with the same email and password.
2. Wait for data to download.
3. Enter your **same PIN** from your old phone (not a new one).

## Before a big exam

- Import roster and answer keys **ahead of time**.
- Print sheets at **100% scale** (Actual size, not Fit to page).
- Do a **trial scan** on 3–5 printed samples.
- Export a backup in Settings.

## After the semester (end of term)

1. Open **Settings** → **End of term** → **Archive a section**.
2. **Sync first** if the app asks — scores must be in the cloud.
3. Pick the finished class (e.g. BSIT-01). It is **removed from this phone only**.
4. View old grades anytime on the **web portal** (Classes → **Archived**).

Use **Delete permanently** only if you want to erase data from the cloud too (rare).

## School year and returning students

- When you **create a section** or **import a roster**, pick the **school year** and **term** (e.g. 2025-2026 · 1st Sem).
- **Archive** last term’s section before reusing the same name (e.g. BSIT-01) for a new roster.
- **Returning students** are matched by **Student ID** (`school_id`) on import — names and sections can update; assign a **new OMR ID** if the sheet number changed.

## Need help?

Contact your school IT or the app developer. Say what screen you were on and what the error message said.
